Bounded business agent

An agent that recovers overdue revenue with control.

SOLVENT helps teams turn unpaid Stripe invoices into structured recovery work: understand the account, choose the right reminder, check message safety, and ask a human before any risky step. The goal is simple: get earned money moving again without turning the process into aggressive collections.

01 / Stripe Invoice enters the graph

Amount, age, hosted payment link, and customer context become structured agent state.

02 / Hermes Recovery move is selected

Nous Hermes chooses the softest stage that can still work: gentle, firm, or hold.

03 / Nemotron Safety boundary checks tone

NVIDIA Nemotron screens pressure, policy risk, and unsafe wording before anything ships.

04 / Human Irreversible steps pause

Escalation, discounts, payment plans, and risky messages wait for explicit approval.

05 / Solvent Payment closes the loop

Stripe test-mode settlement makes the demo observable, repeatable, and judge-friendly.
